Owner biography

Spinn is the founder and head barber of The Black Barbershop Da Nang. He began his career driven by a strong passion for modern men’s grooming, combining clean contemporary styles with traditional barber techniques.

With many years of hands on experience, Spinn focuses on precision, clean execution, and adapting each haircut to the client’s personal style, face shape, and lifestyle. He believes a great haircut should feel natural, comfortable, and easy to maintain, not just look good at the moment.

In addition to working at the barbershop, Spinn provides custom on location haircut services upon request. He accepts private appointments at resorts, hotels, private residences, or other requested locations for clients who value privacy, convenience, or a personalized grooming experience.

Spinn has gained valuable professional experience working with both local and international clients in Hoi An. This experience helped him refine his skills, service mindset, and understanding of different grooming preferences, which he now brings to every service at The Black Barbershop Da Nang.

• SHOP STORY

The Black Barbershop Da Nang is a modern barbershop created for men who appreciate clean cuts, sharp details, and professional grooming. The shop focuses on haircuts, shaving, and complete grooming services delivered with consistency and attention to detail.

The space is designed with a clean and minimal aesthetic, using darker tones to create a calm, comfortable, and masculine atmosphere. Every service is performed with proper barber techniques and a careful working process to ensure quality and reliability.

Beyond the in shop experience, The Black Barbershop also offers flexible grooming solutions. Custom haircut services can be arranged at resorts, hotels, or private locations upon request, making it convenient for travelers, busy professionals, or special occasions.

With experience gained from working in both Da Nang and Hoi An, The Black Barbershop aims to build long term relationships with clients through trust, skill, and a professional service experience.
